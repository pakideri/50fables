Scenic Wayanad

Karlad Lake

16 km from 50 Fables
Recommended: 2 Hours

The Adventure Lakeside

Karlad Lake is one of the most serene and naturally beautiful freshwater lakes in Kerala. Located in Thariode, it is the second largest lake in Wayanad and is famous for its adventure-packed lakeside experience.

Unlike the busier Banasura Sagar Dam, Karlad Lake offers a more intimate connection with nature. It is heavily utilized for adventure sports such as zip-lining across the lake, kayaking, bamboo rafting, and rock climbing. The lake is surrounded by bamboo groves and lush greenery, making it a perfect spot for photography and bird watching.

Travel Tip: Wear comfortable shoes if you plan to walk the perimeter trail or engage in climbing. Zip-lining across the lake is a MUST for adventure seekers.

Adventure Hub

The Wayanad Adventure Camp is located right on the banks of Karlad Lake. Whether you want to quietly paddle a kayak or experience the adrenaline of the longest zip line in South India, this place has something for everyone in the family.

Visitor Essentials

  • Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM
  • Entry Fee: Adults: ₹30 | Activity fees extra.
  • Activities: Zip lining, Kayaking, Boating, Archery.
  • Best Season: October to April.

The road to Karlad Lake is quite scenic. We can arrange a local guide to show you the best viewpoints along the Thariode route.
