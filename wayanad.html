Discover Wayanad

Thirunelli Temple

It is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Maha Vishnu beside the Brahmagiri hills in Kerala.

Tholpetty

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is spread across 344 square kms with four ranges namely Sulthan Bathery, Muthanga, Kurichiat and Tholpetty.

Karlad Lake

Karlad Lake is located in Thariode, which is around 16 km away from Kalpetta.

Kanthampara Falls

A beautiful waterfall situated about 22 km away from Kalpetta.

Chembra Peak

Hike up the rugged terrains of the Chembra Peak located 2,100 metres above sea level on the southern part of Wayanad.

Wayanad's Enchanted Trails

Explore lush trails through the ancient Western Ghats with experienced naturalists who'll share stories of local tribes and the region's rich flora and fauna.

Vast Spice Plantation Routes

Hit adrenaline-pumping trails winding through rolling hills of coffee, cardamom, and pepper, offering thrilling descents and panoramic vistas

The Serene Kabini Backwaters

Glide down scenic backwaters surrounded by wildlife and stunning natural views, offering a peaceful glimpse into rural life near the Kabini River.

Edakkal Cave History

Lay back under a clear, open sky and explore the constellations, or discover the prehistoric rock art and ancient history within the famous Edakkal Caves.
