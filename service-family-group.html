Family & Group Activities

Create Timeless Memories Together

Reconnect with your loved ones in a sanctuary designed for togetherness. At 50 Fables, we offer experiences that transcend generations, ensuring that both the young and the young at heart find joy in every moment.

Our group and family programs are built on the principles of fun, shared discovery, and the simple beauty of communal life. Whether you're planning a massive family reunion or a close-knit getaway with friends, our custom activities are designed to spark conversation and create laughter.

Traditional Games

Revisit your roots with traditional local games like Pallanguzhi, or enjoy modern outdoor board games on our manicured lawns.

Group Dining

Enjoy massive feasts featuring authentic Wayanad cuisine, served under the sprawling canopy of our ancient trees.

Plantation Walks

Easy-paced walks through our spice gardens, perfect for elders and young children alike to learn about nature.

Cultural Evenings

Experience local music, dance, and storytelling sessions around a central fire—a hit for groups of all sizes.

Our resort also provides dedicated areas for group activities, including open-air pavilions and bonfire pits designed to accommodate large groups while maintaining an intimate feel. We believe that the best stories are written together, and 50 fables is where your next chapter begins.
