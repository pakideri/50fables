Natural Pools

Echoes of the Earth's Purest Waters

Step away from the artificial and dive into the primal. At 50 Fables, our natural stone pools are designed to feel like hidden mountain lagoons, filtered by nature and framed by the wild.

Our pools are more than just water features; they are eco-systems of relaxation. Built with locally sourced granite and slate, these pools use organic filtration systems to ensure the water remains as pure as a mountain spring, without the harsh smell of chemicals.

Organic Filtration

We use bio-filtering systems and natural mineral balances to keep the water crystal clear and skin-friendly.

Stone-Clad Design

Rough-hewn natural stones provide a slip-resistant, cooling surface that blends seamlessly with the forest floor.

Moonlight Swim

Ambient, low-intensity lighting allows for a safe and magical swimming experience under the stars.

Infinite Views

Positioned on the edge of the estate, our main pool offers uninterrupted views of the misty valleys beyond.

Whether you're starting your morning with a refreshing dip or cooling off after a trek, our natural pools offer a sensory escape that grounds you. Feel the texture of the earth beneath your feet and hear the distant calls of forest birds as you float in total serenity.

Private Access

Our pools are exclusive to resort guests, ensuring a private and tranquil experience for you and your family.
