Outdoor Adventures

Unleash Your Inner Explorer

Wayanad is a playground for those who seek the thrill of the wild. At 50 Fables, we bring you closer to nature with curated outdoor experiences that blend adventure with the luxury of comfort.

Our outdoor adventure program is designed for everyone—from the casual weekend trekker to the seasoned adrenaline junkie. Nestled in the heart of the Western Ghats, our resort serves as the perfect base camp for discovering hidden waterfalls, misty peaks, and dense forest trails.

Mountain Trekking

Guided treks to Chembra Peak and Banasura Hill with experienced local guides who know every secret trail.

Premium Glamping

Experience the ruggedness of camping without sacrificing comfort. Our luxury tents feature en-suite bathrooms and stunning views.

Night Campfire

Gather around a roaring fire under the star-lit Wayanad sky. Perfect for group bonding and storytelling.

Wildlife Safaris

Exclusive trips to the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary to spot elephants, leopards, and diverse bird species.

Sample Itinerary

A typical adventure day begins with a pre-dawn sunrise trek, followed by a hearty estate-style breakfast. The afternoon is reserved for either a spice plantation walk or a visit to the nearby waterfalls. As dusk falls, we prepare the campfire and setup for a night of stargazing.
